About the Mobility Parking Scheme

The Mobility Parking Scheme (MPS) provides special parking provisions for residents with mobility or vision impairments. Eligible residents can apply for an MPS permit to help them maintain independence and access convenient parking.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the MPS, residents must have a medical condition that severely affects their mobility or vision. The application process involves completing an online form and submitting it for review by a registered medical practitioner.

How to Apply

Residents wishing to apply for an MPS permit must first register online as a customer of the Southern Coastland Roads Authority. Once registered, you can log in to complete the application form.
